Render Order 66 is a Game Development blog about all things Game Dev! We want to write about all aspects of Game Development such as programming challenges, creating art or audio for your games, working within a team, project setup and marketing.

This blog is created by Anthony Reed and Corey Underdown in our spare time. We both love Game Development and want to share our knowledge and findings, helping others to find solutions to all their Game Developemt problems.
